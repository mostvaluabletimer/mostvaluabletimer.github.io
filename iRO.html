Adding a card

Click on to search for the target card (boss and/or instance).

Add time of death or, if instance, its end time. Be sure to convert the time on MVP tombstone to your time zone if it is different from the official server time.

Depending on your browser, you must enter the time in 12h (am/pm) or 24h format, as well as the date in yyyy/mm/dd format if there is no calendar.

Choosing the server and adding the short description are optional, but they can offer a better organization (server/character).

Bosses cards will appear under the 'Bosses' tab, as will instance cards under the 'Instances' tab.

Clearing the section

Click on to clear the section after confirmation.

Warning: this cannot be undone.

Notifications

If you allow MVT to send you notifications, you can receive notifications about events regarding the cards, such as 10 and 5 minutes left, delay, when boss is alive, or when instances can be opened.

Click on to configure which notifications you want to receive.

At the end of the countdown, you may be notified with the MVP sound if you choose.
